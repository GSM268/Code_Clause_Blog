Life Goals

A majority of us have 'life goals’, dreams that we want to work towards and convert into reality. There are the usual life goals like being the best in your chosen field and earning loads of money, fame and respect. Then there are the more noble ones like making discoveries and innovations that change the whole world or serving the country for the betterment of the people. ‘Life goals’ have become a must have thing. Everyone ponders upon them. Life goals are now considered to be one of life's big questions; 'What do I want to achieve by the time I take my last breath?’

Conversation

I lived in Australia for a year. There, everyone had a habit of greeting each other when they met. Every conversation started with a smile and a simple question, 'how are you today?’, and ended with a even bigger grin, a thank you and a 'hope you have a nice day!’. Everyone; family friends, shopkeepers, bus drivers, waiters etc, was treated with the same manners and politeness. I love this little custom.
